Our Legacy

Our Heritage

A century of artisanal excellence and family traditions

1924

The Foundation

Alessandro Lumina opens the first boutique in the heart of Florence, Italy, specializing in hand-forged gold ornaments.

1958

Diamond Era

The second generation introduces rare gemstones and GIA-certified diamonds, setting new standards for quality in European markets.

1988

Global Reach

LUMINA expands to New York's Fifth Avenue, becoming a synonymous name for absolute luxury and bespoke artistry.

2024

The Modern Legacy

Blending 100 years of tradition with modern digital experiences, while maintaining the soul of Italian craftsmanship.

Ethics First

Ethical & Sustainable

We are committed to responsible sourcing and environmental stewardship, ensuring our brilliance never comes at a cost to the planet.

Conflict-Free Diamonds

All our diamonds are sourced through the Kimberley Process, ensuring they are 100% conflict-free and ethically mined.

Recycled Metals

We use recycled gold and platinum in our designs to reduce the environmental impact of traditional mining.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

Our signature boxes are made from sustainable materials and are designed to be kept and cherished for a lifetime.
