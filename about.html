Artisanal Excellence

Every LUMINA piece is a testament to our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship

Handcrafted

Each piece is meticulously crafted by hand using traditional techniques passed down through generations

Quality Control

Rigorous inspection at every stage ensures perfection in every detail and flawless execution

Certified

Ethical & Sustainable

We are committed to responsible sourcing and environmental stewardship, ensuring our brilliance never comes at a cost to the planet.

Conflict-Free Diamonds

All our diamonds are sourced through the Kimberley Process, ensuring they are 100% conflict-free and ethically mined.

We use recycled gold and platinum in our designs to reduce the environmental impact of traditional mining.

Our signature boxes are made from sustainable materials and are designed to be kept and cherished for a lifetime.
