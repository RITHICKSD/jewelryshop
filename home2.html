The Absolute Setting

Discover our proprietary 'Invisible Setting', a secret kept for five generations within the Lumina family atelier. Through painstaking precision and micron-level accuracy, we create a channel where stones stand edge-to-edge, allowing them to float without metal visible to the human eye. This technique requires over 1,200 hours of manual labor to master for a single signature necklace.
