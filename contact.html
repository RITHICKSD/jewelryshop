Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How long does custom jewelry design take?

Our custom jewelry design process typically takes 4-6 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery. This includes design consultation (1 week), material sourcing (1-2 weeks), and craftsmanship (2-3 weeks). Rush orders may be available for an additional fee.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide via secure insured shipping through our trusted partners. International orders typically arrive within 3-7 business days. All packages are fully insured for their full value and require signature upon delivery for security.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for custom pieces and 60-day return policy for ready-to-ship items. Items must be in original condition with all documentation and packaging. Custom pieces require a design approval deposit which is non-refundable after approval.

How do I care for my jewelry?

Regular cleaning with mild soap and warm water, professional cleaning twice yearly, and proper storage in our provided jewelry boxes will maintain your pieces' beauty. Avoid harsh chemicals, extreme temperatures, and store pieces separately to prevent scratching.

Do you provide jewelry appraisals?

Yes, we provide professional appraisal services for insurance and estate purposes. Our certified gemologists offer detailed documentation including color grades, clarity ratings, and current market valuations. Appraisals typically take 3-5 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and offer financing options for qualifying purchases. Payment plans are available for custom designs with 50% deposit required to begin work.

Can you repair jewelry from other brands?

Absolutely! Our master craftsmen can repair and restore jewelry from any brand. We provide detailed estimates before any work begins and stand behind our repairs with a comprehensive warranty covering workmanship and materials.

Do you offer wedding consultations?

Yes, we specialize in wedding jewelry consultations including engagement rings, wedding bands, and anniversary gifts. Our experts help couples select or design pieces that symbolize their unique love story with complimentary consultations.
